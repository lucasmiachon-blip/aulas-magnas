[0:00–0:40] CONTEXT "Before clinical stages, we need the vocabulary. In the early 1990s, the HCV epidemic forced pathologists to standardize fibrosis scoring." [0:40–1:30] METAVIR STRIP — stages appear one by one "The French METAVIR group: F0 to F4. Simple. Universal. Designed for HCV, adopted by everyone. Every hepatologist on earth thinks in F-stages." [1:30–2:20] LIMITATIONS "The problem? Biopsy samples 1/50,000th of the liver. Sampling error is real. Interobserver variability is real.
